# Your packaging data form — now faster to fill

We rebuilt the packaging compliance form around one idea: **you should only ever look at the thing you're working on right now — and never lose sight of everything you've already done.** Here's what's new.

---

## Focus mode — one section at a time
Open a section and every other one quietly steps back, so the page stays short and your eyes go straight to the fields that matter.
*No more scrolling through a wall of eleven open sections looking for where you left off.*

## See your data at a glance — even when collapsed
Every closed section shows a one-line summary of what's inside it — `Flat · 35 · 30`, `Transparent · 1-colour print`, and so on.
*Check your whole submission in seconds without opening a single card.*

## The active card stands out
The section you're filling gets a soft green highlight and full clarity, while the rest fade back to the background.
*You always know exactly where you are — no hunting, no guessing.*

## Nothing is locked — override anything
Auto-populated fields (department, class, ORINs…) start pre-filled, but a single click makes any of them editable.
*Trust the prefill when it's right, fix it in one click when it isn't.*

## Prefill from a similar component
Spotted a near-identical component? One button copies its values across the remaining sections for you to review.
*Turn twenty minutes of typing into a thirty-second check.*

## Live progress that keeps you moving
A running "X of 11 complete" counter, per-phase progress, missing-field badges and a "Next incomplete section" jump keep momentum on your side.
*Always know how much is left — and get there in one click.*

## Fill freely, submit safely
Sections never block you as you work; the only gate is the final submit, which points you straight at anything still missing.
*Work in any order you like, and never get caught out by a hidden required field.*

## Your work is always saved
A quiet "Draft saved" indicator confirms every change is kept as you go.
*Step away whenever you need to — your progress is waiting when you're back.*

---

**In short:** less scrolling, less re-typing, and a clear view of what's done and what's left — so submitting compliant packaging data is quick, confident, and hard to get wrong.
