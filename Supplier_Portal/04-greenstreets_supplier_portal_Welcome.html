Welcome, Luntai Packaging Co.

Primark Stores Ltd has asked you to declare the packaging you supply, so it can meet its EU & UK packaging compliance obligations (EPR & PPWR). This portal walks you through it in three steps — start by building a library of your packaging components, then match them to the products Primark buys from you.

1

Build your packaging component library Step 1

Import a list of the packaging components you produce — swing tags, poly bags, cartons, pallets and so on. You can upload a spreadsheet or let the AI read your spec sheets and build the list for you.

2

Assign packaging to products Step 2

For each product Primark requested, assign the packaging components it ships in. This is what tells Primark exactly what packaging goes onto every product they buy from you.

3

Review & track compliance Step 3

Check the compliance status of each component, resolve anything flagged for review, and download Declarations of Conformity. You're done — the portal keeps everything up to date.
